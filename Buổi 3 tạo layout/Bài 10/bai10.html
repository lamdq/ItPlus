Arsenal Football Club ( abbreviated: Arsenal FC) is a professional football club based in Holloway, London, currently playing in the English Premier League, Highest league in the English football system. Arsenal have won 13 national championships, 13 FA Cups (record), 2 English League Cups, 1 League League Cup, 15 English Super Cups, 1 UEFA Cup and 1 Inter-Cities Fairs Cup. Arsenal is the club with the second highest number of wins in English football history, and is also the only English team so far to win the national championship with an unbeaten record through 38 rounds. named The Invincibles (Vietnamese: The unbeaten team) and won the English Premier League Cup.
